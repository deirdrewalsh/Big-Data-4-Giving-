Smart Giving

This project aims to provide community-level data to donors and organizations that provide services. Started at ATXHack4Change 2016, we take census data to identify areas of need in our city to allow donors and non-profits to make informed decisions on how their dollars and efforts can impact our community. The initial product focuses on the city of Austin, Texas and census data on poverty.

Started with low poverty index.
